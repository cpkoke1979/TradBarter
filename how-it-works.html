How It Works

1. Create Your Profile

Set up your barter profile with details about your skills, services, and items available for trade.

2. Browse Listings

Find listings from others in your area or beyond. Discover items or services you need.

3. Propose a Trade

Message users directly and suggest a fair trade. You can negotiate or counter-offer with ease.

4. Agree & Trade

Once both parties agree, carry out the swap. Leave reviews to build community trust.
