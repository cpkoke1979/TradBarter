How It Works

1. Create Your Profile

Set up your free barter profile with details about your skills, services, and items available for trade. You’ll also be asked to verify your identity by uploading a document, which helps ensure a safe and trusted community.

2. Browse Listings

Explore all available listings from across the platform — no login required. You’ll see what people are offering and what they’re looking for. Once you log in, the platform will highlight matched listings—showing you users who have exactly what you want and want what you have. These personalised matches help you find ideal trading partners faster and with more accuracy.

3. Propose a Trade

Message users directly using our built-in tools and suggest a fair trade. You can negotiate or counter-offer with ease. Discuss whether the exchange will be online, in-person, or via local drop-off—whatever suits both parties.

4. Agree & Trade

Once both parties agree, carry out the swap in your preferred way. After completion, leave a review to help build a strong, trusted community. Ratings and honest feedback ensure fairness and encourage positive growth across the platform.
